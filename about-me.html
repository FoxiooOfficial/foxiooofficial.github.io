About me!


Hi, I'm Foxioo and I'm from Poland 🇵🇱.
The languages I know are, of course, Polish and English (Something between level A2 and B1 but more A2).
My birthday is May 4.



What do I like?
- Programming; I know HLSL, GLSL, C++, C (basics), Python (basics), JavaScript (absolute basics)
- Drawing
What don't I like?
- Loud noises and things that are super distracting (;w; I WANT TO FOCUS AAAAA)

My first exposure to programming was in early elementary school; I watched tutorials on how to write programs in C++, but it was too hard, so I switched from C++ to Visual Basic and made my first projects or games in Microsoft Visual Studio 2010 Express.
Back then, it was clear how I stood out from the rest-instead of messing around with Scratch or drawing in MS Paint like the other kids in elementary school.
My mom used to tell me I was always "creating" on computer.

Currently, I'm writing shaders for the Clickteam Fusion engine and working on smaller and larger projects within it.
I've experimented with various game engines, from Unity to GameMaker Studio 8.1 (there were other lesser-known engines along the way), but Fusion won me over with its simplicity (I've been using Fusion since 2019).

According to a psychologist, I'm on the autism spectrum with ADHD (I believe him ¯\_(ツ)_/¯)

My guiding principle is to design projects so they run on the oldest hardware possible while still supporting newer solutions (these days, companies and corporations artificially age our hardware so we must replace it with new models.... ugh.)
For example, this website is optimized to run on a phone from 2012.
If my requests to separate effects from D3D8 and D3D9 are added to Fusion, I will also implement this idea in FSP (To make all my effects work on EVERY RUNTIME).
