My games

Here you'll find a list of games I'm currently working on or have worked on:

  • Exadelta

    I’ve been working on this game for several years now. At first, it was supposed to be an Undertale fangame called "Sparetale," where the goal was simply to spare your enemies. The project has evolved, and for the past 6 or 7 years, I’ve been developing a story with my own characters. The game won’t be released anytime soon because I have too much on my plate, but at the same time, I’m putting the story together in my head.

    Exadelta is meant to be an RPG with a more developed combat system, similar to Undertale, combined with platforming elements.

    "A long, long time ago, when the gods were creating the world together, a conflict arose with one of them; he created a race that was meant to be both weak and strong in an act of rebellion, but this god was killed by the other gods too late, as the race he created had already spread across the world. In the present day, you are a student unaware of your own race, wondering for years why your loved ones disappear without a trace.
    The goddess "Evee," along with "Lazur," are doing everything they can to rid the planet of the two stars of this race.
    On her way home, "something” tries to kill the main character; while fleeing, she falls into a portal that transports her to the neighboring country/kingdom of "Exadelta," where she must escape and survive."

    It is currently unavailable.
